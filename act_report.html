Introduction¶

The dataset used in this analysis was taken from WeRateDogs Twitter Account. It was gathered using multiple resources and methods. The data was then assessed for quality and tidiness issues. Later on, cleaning operations were done on the data in order to provide useful insights through our analysis.

Storing, Analyzing, Visualizing WeRateDogs Cleaned Data¶

During the cleaning operations, I have successfully stored all the clean information in a DataFrame. The final clean dataframe contained 1679 entries while the original dataset contained 2356. This final data was exported to a csv file named 'twitter_archive_master.csv'

Thereafter, I have analyzed the data for the most popular dog breeds. The top five breeds were Golden Retriever, Labrador Retriever, Pembroke, Chihuahua and Pug. Golden Retriever topped the list with occurance in 158 entries in the dataset.
Further, I looked best rated breeds of dogs. The best rated dog breed was found to be Bouvier des Flanders. But the average ratings of top dog breeds are pretty close to one another.

Conclusions¶

The data from WeRateDogs was taken from a real world scenario which rarely comes clean. After successfully wrangling the data and performing the analysis, the major findings were as follows:

  • The most tweeted breed was found to be Golden Retriever.
  • The best rated breed was Bouvier des Flanders.
  • There is a weak positive correlation between dog ratings and retweet count.
  • The number of tweets over time have consistently been decreasing.
